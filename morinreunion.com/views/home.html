Ticket price

Family members 21 years+ will need to purchase tickets. The ticket price for adults will be $65, paid directly to your family's representative. The family reunion is free for all family members under 21 years.

Food

Lunch and dinner is free on June 28th. Uncle Lucien has graciously paid for all food costs. Food will be prepared by Ana Esposito. Thank you, Uncle Lucien and Ana, and thanks to Jim for the use of your home!

In addition to food provided please bring your favorite Traditional Family Dishes that can be shared with your cousins, aunts and uncles. There’s nothing better than a family reunion except a family reunion with home cooking!

Alcoholic Beverages

There will a keg of beer for all to enjoy. It’s a BYOB party for those who want to drink wine and/or hard liquor.
